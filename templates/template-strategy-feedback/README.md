# Strategy-Feedback Plugin

A strategy pressure-testing toolkit that works for any company. Five skills that work together to help you think through product decisions, competitive positioning, pricing, market entry, and strategic bets — before you commit.

## What's Inside

**Strategy Council** — Activates 3-5 advisory personas (investors, operators, buyers, builders, finance) to debate the strategic merits of your question. Use when the question is "should we build this, price this, or pursue this?"

**Buyer Persona Council** — Activates 3-5 buyer voices to pressure-test how your messaging, pitch, or campaign lands. Use when the question is "how does this land with buyers?"

**Strategy Frameworks** — Fourteen analytical frameworks you can apply to any strategic question. Organized into five categories: Advantage (7 Powers, Porter's Five Forces, Aggregation Theory, Moats & Flywheels, Leaky Bucket), Product (Jobs to Be Done, Blue Ocean / Value Curve), Growth (Crossing the Chasm, SaaS vs. Marketplace, Reverse-Engineered P&L), Decision (Disruptive Innovation, Three Horizons, MECE Issue Trees), and Synthesis (Minto Pyramid Principle).

**Competitive Intel** — A living context layer with your company's competitive landscape, major strategic decisions, and market context. Grounding for all other skills. Supports multiple companies.

**Strategy Orchestrator** — The router and sequencer. Helps you pick the right tool for your question, or runs a multi-step strategy session when you need the full analysis.

## Multi-Company Support

This plugin supports one or more company contexts. Each company gets its own set of reference files (competitive landscape, strategic decisions, market context, personas). When you have multiple companies configured, the plugin asks which context to use. When you have one, it loads automatically. When you have none, it works generically and offers to set up company context.

## Quick Start

1. Install the plugin
2. Say: "Set up a new company for [your company name]"
3. Walk through the guided setup (company profile, competitors, strategy, personas)
4. Start asking strategic questions

See `SETUP.md` for detailed setup instructions.

## How to Invoke

**Direct invocation examples:**

- "Council: evaluate this pricing model" — Strategy Council
- "Persona council: review this outbound sequence" — Buyer Persona Council
- "Apply 7 Powers to our product" — Strategy Frameworks
- "What's our competitive landscape?" — Competitive Intel
- "Strategy session: should we expand to healthcare?" — Orchestrator

**Framework triggers:**

- "Run 7 Powers on [topic]"
- "MECE this question"
- "Minto this recommendation"
- "What framework should I use for [question]?"

## Maintenance

The councils and frameworks are durable — they don't go stale. The competitive intel layer does. See `MAINTENANCE.md` for the full guide.
