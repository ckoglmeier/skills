## Research Preferences

**Who reads research outputs:** [Executive team? Sales? Product? Strategy?
Knowing the audience helps calibrate depth and tone.]

**Output format preferences:**
- Prose vs. bullets: Prose-first. Use bullets only for genuinely enumerable items.
  Never bullets for analysis or argument.
- Length: Thorough when the decision warrants it. Don't pad, but don't truncate substance.
- Tone: Direct and opinionated. State a clear point of view. Surface tensions and
  tradeoffs explicitly. Don't hedge.
- Other: Every quantitative claim needs an inline source URL. End with concrete
  implications for the decision at hand.
